The Artist

Miles Walker

An illustrator and world builder based in San Antonio, Texas. My work lives at the intersection of the real and the fantastical — familiar landscapes transformed by creatures that shouldn't exist, cities that hum with quiet magic, and the strange logic of places that never were.

I build worlds that feel lived-in. Every piece is a window into the same sprawling universe — one where kaiju sun themselves by the River Walk, drakes haunt the foundry district, and potions are sold out of hand-painted roadside shacks on the way out of town.

Available for commissions, licensing, and collaborative projects. Especially interested in TTRPG illustration, cover art, and projects with deep lore to dig into.
